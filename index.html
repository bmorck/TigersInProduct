What We Do

Hands-on Experience

We match students with our partnered companies in areas of Product Management, Software Engineering, and UI/UX Design. Students can get involved in semester long projects with our partners. With these projects,we aim to ease the transition for students into roles within industry or in developing their own products.

One-on-One Mentorship

We connect students with professionals within industry to help them better understand the nature of their position, the challenges they face, and how to approach the recruiting process.

Workshops

We host workshops led by Product Managers that share their experiences in their work and guide students through how they got involved in their career.
